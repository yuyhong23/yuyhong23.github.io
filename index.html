About Me

About


Hi, this is Yuying, a data analyst who is passionate about net zero carbon economy. I was born in Guangdong, China and grew up in San Francisco.

Education & Career Interest


I graduated from UC Berkeley in May 2019 with a dual degree in Environmental Economics and Policy and Asian Studies.

After graduation, while pondering my next move in life, I worked as an operations associate at Foodnet Supermarket Inc from October 2019 to February 2021. During my time at Foodnet Supermarket, I was exposed to various operational tasks, such as marketing, operating POS (point of sale) system backends, and managing newly launched E-commerce platforms. Thanks to my experience at Foodnet Supermarket, I realized that I am surprisingly comfortable working with technologies and is interested in data analytics.

After realizing my newfound career interest, I decided to attend UC Berkeley Extension Data Analytics and Visualization Boot Camp (from October 2020 to April 2021) to acquire the skills needed to become a data analyst.

Currently, I am looking for a full-time data analytst/consultant position to utilize and further develop my skills. Since climate change is an issue that I am especially concerned about, I am looking specifically into the environmental related sector for job opportunities. For the prospects of our world, I believe that our economy needs to transition into a net zero carbon economy swiftly; thus, I am eager to apply my data analytics skills to assist such transition.
